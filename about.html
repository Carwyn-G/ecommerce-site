About Rooliyay Creations

Rooliyay Creations is run by longtime-crafter Squid. They first learned to sew at seven years old making doll clothes and other small projects with their grandmother. Since childhood they've been developing their sewing and crafting skills and have dabbled in all sorts of fabric and fiber arts. It wasn't until the pandemic that they expanded their sewing skills to puff quilting and hexagon quilting, but quickly fell in love with the craft. Since then they opened Rooliyay Creations to share their crafts with the world.

In addition to crafting and cross-stitching, Squid has long been dedicated to supporting mental health initiatives and particularly LGBTQ mental health. They volunteered with the American Foundation for Suicide Prevention from 2013 to 2024, including as a member and later co-chief of the LGBTQ action committee for AFSP Tennessee. It is still a cause that is near and dear to their heart. Though their volunteer role with AFSP has currently been put on hold while they attend university abroad, they remain passionate about supporting mental health and speaking out about suicide.

For now their crafting has slowed as they attend university in order to learn the skills necessary to build this website, but in between classes and homework they still spend much of their free time with a craft project in their hands.
